About

1.4 billion People in the world have no access to power and 400 million of them live in rural India.Deviab was founded with a vision to eradicate energy inequity in India and our mission is to provide reliable and affordable energy services to low income rural households. We promote clean energy access in rural India by synergizing technology, microfinance and community engagement.
